MPLS Farmers Market Rules

Masks & Social Distancing

Please be prepared to follow the Covid-19 safety guidelines prepared by the CDC, Governor Waltz and the City of Minneapolis. Customers are not required to wear a mask.

Dogs

Only service animals are allowed. There are two issues with dogs (and pets) at the farmers' markets: one is food safety; the other is personal safety.

How can you help us

- Bring your own reusable shopping bag

- Bring smaller bills to offer vendors exact change

- If you feel sick stay home
