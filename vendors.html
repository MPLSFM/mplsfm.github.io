Our market vendors’ attendance and stall assignments vary on a daily basis and depend on the seasonality of their product. We host vendors that have a legacy of over 5 generations of farming and also allow space for new businesses to get their start at our market. Browse our list of vendors below to learn more.

Interested in becoming a vendor? Contact our general manager at info@mplsfarmersmarket.com. We are currently accepting applications for local growers.
