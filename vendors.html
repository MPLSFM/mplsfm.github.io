Check out our vendors for the 2023 season

Our market vendors’ attendance and stall assignments vary on a daily basis and depend on the seasonality of their products. We host vendors that have a legacy of over 5 generations of farming and also allow space for new businesses to get their start at our market. Browse our list of vendors below to learn more.

Interested in becoming a vendor? We are currently accepting applications for local growers. If you want more information, please visit the market office during operational hours and ask for a manager.
